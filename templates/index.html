SchistoTarget is a machine-learning (SVM) classifier for the identification of immunoreactive proteins.

The classifier uses Support Vector Machine (SVM) to discriminate between two categories of proteins. Recently, it has been demonstrated that such a amino acid feature based classifier can significantly increase prediction accuracy. The protien SVM predictions are highly valuable for identifying genes important for host-parasite interaction and the discovery of novel drug and vaccine targets.



  • IgEPredicts if the proteins are IgE reactive or not
  • IgG1Predicts if the proteins are IgG1 reactive or not
    Immuno-reactivityPredicts the test proteins immunoreactive or not
  • IgG3Predicts if the proteins are IgG3 reactive or not
  • IgG4Predicts if the proteins are IgG4 reactive or not
